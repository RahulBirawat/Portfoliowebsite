UI(User Interface) and UX(User Experience) Designing is also a technology to make the website more beautiful and attractive.UI UX designers create the user interface for an app, website, or other interactive media. Their work includes collaborating with product managers and engineers to gather requirements from users before designing ideas that can be communicated using storyboards. They also process flows or sitemaps.UI designers design the visual elements of a product that users interact with, while UX designers make sure that the design is aligned with the user needs. UI/UX designer is an in-demand job with lots of companies looking to hire for this profile.
